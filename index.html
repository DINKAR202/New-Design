About Us

Welcome to MS Ramaiah Institute of Management

Ramaiah Institute of Management (RIM) is a private business school located in Bangalore, India. The institute is affiliated with the M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (MSRUAS) and is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).RIM has a strong placement record with top companies across various sectors such as banking, consulting, FMCG, IT, and healthcare visiting the campus for recruitment. The average salary package offered to students during the 2020-2021 placement season was around INR 7.2 lakhs per annum, while the highest package offered was INR 13 lakhs per annum.

Campus Life

Campus Life

✅The campus is spread across 3 acres and is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment for students.

✅ Infrastructure:MSRIM has a state-of-the-art infrastructure with modern classrooms, well-equipped computer labs, a well-stocked library, and a sports complex.

✅ Student Clubs:   MSRIM has various student clubs that cater to the interests of students. These   clubs organize events, workshops, and competitions related to their respective fields. Some of the popular student clubs at MSRIM are the Marketing Club, HR Club, Finance Club, and Club.

✅ Events and Competitions: MSRIM hosts various events and competitions throughout the year. Some of the popular events include the National Management Fest, Cultural Fest, Sports Fest, and International Conference on Management.

✅ Guest Lectures: MSRIM invites renowned speakers and industry experts to deliver guest lectures to students. These lectures provide students with valuable insights into the industry and help them stay updated with the latest trends and practices.

✅ Placements: MSRIM has a dedicated placement cell that helps students secure internships and job placements. The institute has tie-ups with various leading companies, and students get the opportunity to interact with industry professionals during placement drives.

✅ Hostel: The college has hostels with dormitories for students who require on-campus accommodation, with facilities such as mess, common rooms, and study areas.

✅ Sports Facilities: Sports facilities such as playgrounds, indoor sports arenas, and gymnasiums is available to promote physical fitness and sports activities among students.

✅ Computer Center: A dedicated computer center with state-of-the-art computing facilities, internet access, and software for academic and research purposes is available.

✅ Cafeteria: A cafeteria or canteen providing food and refreshments is available on the campus, offering meals, snacks, and beverages for students and staff.

✅ Transportation: Transportation facilities such as buses orvans to facilitate commuting for students and staff is provided.

✅ Medical Facilities: Medical Facilities inside the campus are available to provide medical facilities and emergency services to students and staff.

✅ Wi-Fi and Internet Access: The college provide Wi-Fi and internet access across the campus to facilitate research, learning, and communication.
